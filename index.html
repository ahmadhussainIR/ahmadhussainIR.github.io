About

I am a U.S. International Medical Graduate currently working as a postdoctoral research fellow at Harvard Medical School and Brigham and Women’s Hospital. My work focuses on clinical research and imaging-based innovation in interventional radiology.

Research Focus

  • Genicular Artery Embolization (GAE)
  • Embolization therapies in musculoskeletal and vascular disease
  • Quantitative angiographic and perfusion imaging
  • Clinical trial design and outcomes research

Current Work

My current research is centered on developing objective imaging biomarkers to evaluate procedural success in embolization therapies. This includes studying vascular flow dynamics and their relationship with patient outcomes, with the goal of improving treatment standardization and effectiveness.
